About Us

Welcome to Read Buddy, an innovative web-based learning platform designed to help elementary students improve their English reading comprehension. Our mission is to make reading fun, interactive, and accessible for every learner.

Through engaging story-based exercises, comprehension quizzes, and activities, Read Buddy supports both teachers and students in developing stronger reading skills. We believe learning should be enjoyable — our platform brings stories to life to spark curiosity and imagination.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Read Buddy?

Read Buddy is a web-based learning tool that helps students improve their English reading comprehension through interactive stories and quizzes.

Who can use Read Buddy?

It is designed primarily for elementary students, but teachers and parents can also use it to support reading development.

Can I track my reading progress?

Yes. Each user has a personal dashboard where progress, scores, and achievements are displayed.

Can teachers create their own reading activities?

Yes. Teachers can upload reading materials, create comprehension questions, and assign them to students.

What devices can I use to access Read Buddy?

You can use a computer, tablet, or smartphone with an internet connection and a web browser.

Is my data safe on the website?

Absolutely. We value your privacy and ensure that all user data is securely stored and protected.
